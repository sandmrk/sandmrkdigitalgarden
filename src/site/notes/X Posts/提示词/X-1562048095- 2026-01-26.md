# 这期分享一套「新中式工笔」提示词。

### 💡 核心观点
这期分享一套「新中式工笔」提示词。它彻底拒绝了AI常见的油腻写实感，而是像在温润宣纸上揉开了淡墨，又用极细金线锁住了神魂。空灵、清透、甚至能闻到纸本的呼吸声。

🎨 视觉逻辑 (Visual Core)
宣纸半哑光 (Rice Paper Matte) 拒绝塑料光泽。皮肤透光但不反光，保留极其微小的纸张颗粒感。
留白与秩序 (The Power of White) 典型的中心均衡构图，给画面留出足够的“气”。背景极简，拉满疏离感。
笔触：工笔描金 (Gongbi Gold) 用极细的金线（Cloisonné line）勾勒衣纹。金是点睛，不是堆叠，尽显贵气。
色调：冷调莫兰迪 (Cold Morandi) 主打天青、豆脂、墨灰。低饱和度，极其耐看，哪怕看久了也不会视觉疲劳。

提示词👇
【Subject】
Use “{Character / Theme}” as the single, clear visual subject.
The subject should convey a refined, calm, and restrained classical Chinese aesthetic.
Elegant, quiet, ethereal presence, recognizable at first glance but never flashy or overstated.

【Composition】
Half-body portrait, vertical or square format.
Centered and balanced composition, slightly symmetrical.
Subject occupies approximately 60–70% of the frame.
Generous headroom and breathing space.
Low background detail density, high visual clarity on the subject.

【Pose & Expression】
Natural, composed posture; shoulders relaxed.
Facing forward or very slight angle.
Expression is calm, serene, introspective, emotionally restrained.
Eyes looking forward or slightly downward.
Subtly upturned outer eye corners.
Lips gently closed or slightly parted, no teeth visible.
Low emotional intensity, quiet elegance.

【Face & Features (if applicable)】
Realistic proportions, classical Chinese facial structure.
NOT anime, NOT exaggerated.
Soft almond-shaped eyes, minimal eyelid emphasis.
Facial features defined with very few strokes.
Low facial contrast, extremely soft shading.

【Skin & Makeup】
Soft matte, translucent skin, like rice paper.
No oily shine, no strong highlights.
Very light blush diffused on cheeks.
Barely-there eye makeup in warm neutral tones.
Natural brows, light and understated.
Soft bean-paste lip color, muted and elegant.

【Hair & Ornaments (if applicable)】
Traditional Chinese updo or neatly arranged classical hairstyle.
Hair texture natural but simplified.
Hair ornaments are delicate and minimal.
Small floral hairpins, subtle pearl or tassel earrings.
Gold accents used sparingly, never dominant.

【Costume & Patterns】
Traditional Chinese clothing or classical robes.
Primary colors: muted celadon green, pale jade, soft blue-green.
Fabric appears soft and flowing.
Patterns rendered as extremely fine golden linework.
Decorative details are light, sparse, and elegant.

【Background】
Layered distant mountains in pale blue-green tones.
Misty atmosphere with stylized traditional cloud motifs.
Strong sense of air and depth created through fading layers.
Large blank areas and intentional negative space.
Background remains soft, quiet, and unobtrusive.

【Lighting & Rendering】
Soft, diffused ambient lighting.
No hard shadows, no dramatic directional light.
Overall low contrast and gentle tonal transitions.
Watercolor light wash with soft bleeding edges.
Fine-line gongbi-style line art.
Subtle rice paper texture visible throughout.
Low saturation Morandi color palette.

【Style Keywords】
modern Chinese gongbi illustration,
new Chinese watercolor style,
fine line art,
light color wash,
elegant, restrained, serene, ethereal,
clean composition,
high aesthetic quality,
minimalist classical mood.

【Negative Prompt】
anime, manga, chibi, big eyes, exaggerated facial proportions,
high saturation, neon colors, strong contrast, hard shadows,
dramatic rim light, cinematic lighting,
oily skin, glossy highlights, plastic skin, visible pores,
photorealistic rendering, hyper-detailed skin,
oil painting, thick brush strokes, impasto texture,
busy background, clutter, modern clothing,
street fashion, sharp outlines, over-sharpening,
noise, artifacts, watermark, logo, text.
