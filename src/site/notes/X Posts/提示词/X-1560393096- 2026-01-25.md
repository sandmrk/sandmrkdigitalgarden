### 💡 核心观点
📐 AI生图构图篇：视线阻断 (Sightline Obstruction)
为什么有的图看一眼就忘，有的图却让人想"探头去看看"？
区别在于：你是在展示，还是在偷窥。
视线阻断——通过前景的"软遮挡"，强迫观众的视线绕过障碍物才能看到主体，瞬间建立画面的纵深感和故事张力。
【核心原理】
这种构图利用了人类的窥探本能 (Voyeuristic Instinct)：
前景遮挡 (Foreground Block)：放置一个虚化的前景物体（树叶/窗帘/百叶窗），遮挡 30%-50% 的画面。
聚焦缺口 (Focus Pocket)：主体必须出现在遮挡物的"缝隙"或"缺口"中。
视线路径：观众视线被前景阻断 -> 寻找缺口 -> 锁定主体。这个过程比直接看更有趣。
【通用公式】
Cinematic shot, looking through [前景遮挡物], obstructed view.
[主体] seen through the gap, [动作/神态].
Foreground [遮挡物] is out of focus (bokeh), framing the subject.
Depth of field, storytelling atmosphere.

【实战案例】
案例 1：心动暗恋 (The Crush)
适用：日系、校园、清新入像。营造"偷偷注视"的暧昧感。
**Subject**: A girl reading a book in the library, unaware of the camera.
**Foreground/Obstruction**: Looking through gaps in **bookshelves**, blurred books in the foreground framing her face.
**Vibe**: Voyeuristic but innocent, soft lighting, school romance anime style, shallow depth of field.
--ar 3:4
案例 2：悬疑侦探 (The Stakeout)
适用：赛博朋克、黑色电影、悬疑片。营造"监视"或"隔绝"感。
**Subject**: A detective standing in a rainy alley, lit by neon signs.
**Foreground/Obstruction**: View through **rain-streaked glass** or wet car window, partially obscuring the view with water droplets and reflections.
**Vibe**: Neo-noir, mystery, moody, cinematic lighting, bokeh rain.
--ar 16:9
案例 3：自然窥探 (The Reveal)
适用：森系、奇幻、古风。营造"发现秘境"的感觉。
**Subject**: A deer (or elf) drinking water at a forest stream.
**Foreground/Obstruction**: Heavily blurred **green fern leaves** and **branches** in the immediate foreground, creating a natural frame.
**Vibe**: National Geographic style, secret discovery, lush detail, sunlight filtering through leaves.
--ar 4:5
案例 4：人海孤岛 (The Crowd)
适用：街头摄影、都市人文。营造"闹中取静"或"迷失"感。
**Subject**: A person standing still in the middle of a busy Tokyo crossing, looking directly at the camera.
**Foreground/Obstruction**: Blurred **silhouettes of passing pedestrians** (shoulders/heads) blocking the view intermittently.
**Vibe**: Isolation in a crowd, cinematic snapshot, motion blur on foreground, shallow depth of field.
--ar 16:9
【避坑指南】
❌ 别挡眼睛：除非你故意要造悬念，否则遮挡物千万别挡住主体的"眼睛"。眼神是交流的关键。
❌ 必须虚化：前景遮挡物必须是焦外 (Out of Focus) 的。如果前景太清晰，会和主体抢夺注意力。
❌ 不要封死：要留出足够的"气孔"，遮挡是为了衬托，不是为了把人埋起来。
